3D Printing Micro-UAVs

Cyber-Human-Physical Systems Lab, Davis, CA, USA

ID: KM32

Relevant Skills: dynamics, control, software, materials, mechanical design

The goal of the project is to use 3D printing to build a VTOL (vertical takeoff and landing) aircraft that is capable of performing some basic maneuvers, such as hovering.
